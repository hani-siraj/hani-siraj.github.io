Mechanic — Memorial Day
  • Step 1 is the conversion event. Email captured = success regardless of whether they finish step 2.
  • Step 2 enrichment only. Skip link visible — forced SMS gates kill email completion.
  • Tag email subscribers with memorial-day-2026-early-access.
  • Suppress this pop-up post-submission and for the duration of the sale.
Mechanic — Evergreen BAU
  • Frame 1 captures ZPD before any contact info — primes the user, raises completion rate, and tags the profile for goal-based welcome flow branching.
  • Email is the conversion event. Tag goal as a profile property (primary_goal = sleep / energy / skin / recovery).
  • SMS frame must show the skip link clearly. Forced SMS gates kill total opt-in volume.
  • Frame 4 reveals the discount but the actual code is delivered via email. Forces inbox open and trains the subscriber to check email from day one.
