THE LOHNR CARE GUIDE

Our sets are designed for 500+ washes (seriously). Here's how to maximize their life:

WASHING

✓ Cold water only (hot water breaks down cotton fibers)
✓ Turn inside out (protects color + reduces pilling)
✓ Gentle cycle or hand wash (keeps softness intact)
✗ Skip fabric softener (it creates buildup—our cotton is already soft!)

DRYING

✓ Air dry flat = best (preserves shape)
✓ Low heat tumble = fine (remove while slightly damp)
✗ High heat = never (causes shrinkage + fiber damage)

STORAGE

✓ Fold (don't hang pants—stretches waistband)
✓ Breathable drawers (skip plastic bins)
✓ Rotate sets (extends life by reducing wash frequency)
