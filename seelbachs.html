The Brand

A craft spirits retailer where limited-edition bottles sell out in minutes.

Seelbach's specialises in curated craft spirits, particularly rare and limited-edition bourbons, whiskeys, and fine liquors. They cater to serious connoisseurs and regularly run exclusive email drops featuring new lineups that sell out almost instantly.

The Challenge

Automated emails were pushing products that were already gone.

Because Seelbach's inventory moves so fast, their automated abandonment flows (browse, cart, and checkout) were frequently promoting products that had already sold out. A customer would browse a rare bourbon, leave the site, receive an abandonment email hours later, click through, and land on an out-of-stock page. It was eroding trust and wasting high-intent traffic.

The Strategy

Real-time stock checks at the moment of email open, not at the moment of send.

Dynamic stock integration. I built a custom product feature using RetainIQ's dynamic widget functionality that checked Shopify stock levels in real time at the moment the subscriber opened the email, not when it was sent. If a product had sold out between send and open, the email creative would dynamically update to show an "Out of Stock" tag and push the subscriber toward similar products in the same category.

Social proof and urgency layering. I paired the stock integration with live social proof elements showing remaining stock counts and product ratings. For a brand where scarcity is the selling point, showing "3 left in stock" next to a 4.8-star rating turned browsing intent into purchase urgency.

Key Takeaway

The email wasn't broken. The product data was stale.

Seelbach's abandonment flows had good open rates and solid copy. The problem was that the product being shown no longer existed by the time the subscriber opened the email. By shifting the stock check from send-time to open-time, we turned a frustration point into the highest-converting flow in the account. The browse abandonment conversion rate alone increased by 3,789%.
