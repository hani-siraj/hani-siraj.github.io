About

Seelbach's specialises in offering a curated selection of craft spirits, particularly rare and limited-edition bourbons, whiskeys, and other fine liquors. Catering to serious connoisseurs, they frequently conduct exclusive email drops featuring new lineups that often sell out almost instantly.

Challenge faced

Seelbach's automated email flows (browse abandonment, cart abandonment, checkout abandonment) often reminded users about products that were already out of stock, leading to user frustration and missed opportunities.

Solutions implemented

Real-Time Stock Check

To address this issue, I devised a custom product feature built on RetainIQ's dynamic widget functionality. This feature ensured that the product creative in the email was updated in real-time to reflect the current stock status just before the user opened the email. If a product was out of stock, the creative would update to reflect an Out of Stock tag and push users to products in the same category.

Product block dynamically updates based off live stock count from Shopify

Social Proof Integration

I combined this capability with social proof elements, showing remaining stock and product ratings to enhance user trust and create urgency.
