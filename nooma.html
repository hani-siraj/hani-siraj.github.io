About

NOOMA is known for its unique, all-natural hydration drinks, competing in the crowded health drink market. They wanted to establish themselves as the go-to choice for health-conscious consumers.

Challenge faced

NOOMA needed to enhance their email marketing to both attract new customers and retain them, thereby increasing Customer Lifetime Value.

Market analysis and competitive positioning

I began with a thorough market study to understand NOOMA's audience and competitors. This helped identify key Unique Selling Propositions that could convert users. My research found that meta ads comparing NOOMA to industry leaders were most effective.

Solutions implemented

Enhanced Welcome Series

Using this insight, I restructured the welcome series to feature in-depth product comparisons personalised to the user's last viewed product. Paired with a strong first-time discount, this strategy boosted new customer acquisition by 35%.

Dynamic based on what the user browsed last

Educational and Engaging Email Campaigns

To improve retention, I crafted a strategy and content calendar focusing on educational emails about the benefits of NOOMA's ingredients and their health advantages. This content was interspersed with engaging snippets from their Instagram feed, appealing to their health-conscious, upper-middle-class demographic.

Personalised Post-Purchase Flow

I implemented a detailed, personalised post-purchase flow that educated users and provided timely reorder reminders. This strategy ensured ongoing engagement and informed customers about the continuous benefits of the product they purchased.

What this meant to the brand

By personalising email marketing strategies and combining educational and engaging content, I helped NOOMA enhance their Customer Lifetime Value and strengthen their market presence. This approach not only increased new customer acquisition but also boosted customer loyalty, ensuring sustainable growth for the brand.
