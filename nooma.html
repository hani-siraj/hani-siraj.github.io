The Brand

An all-natural hydration drink brand fighting for shelf space in a crowded market.

NOOMA makes all-natural hydration drinks and competes in the packed health drink space. They wanted to establish themselves as the go-to choice for health-conscious consumers, but their email programme was focused almost entirely on acquisition with little attention paid to what happened after the first purchase.

The Challenge

Plenty of first-time buyers. Not enough of them came back.

NOOMA needed to improve both acquisition and retention, with the ultimate goal of increasing customer lifetime value. Their meta ads were performing well for new customer acquisition, but the email side wasn't doing its job in converting those new buyers into repeat customers and subscribers.

The Strategy

Competitive positioning at entry, education in the middle, and timed reorder nudges at the end.

Restructured welcome series. I rebuilt the welcome flow to feature in-depth product comparisons personalised to the user's last browsed product. The meta ads were already running comparison-style creative that was converting well, so I carried that messaging through into email to keep the narrative consistent from ad click to inbox.

Educational content campaigns. Built a content stream focused on ingredient benefits and health advantages, pulling in Instagram content to build brand affinity. The goal was to move subscribers from "I bought this once" to "I understand why this is better than what I was drinking before."

Personalised post-purchase flow. Implemented a detailed post-purchase sequence that educated users on their specific product, then triggered timed reorder reminders based on estimated consumption cycles. The flow adapted based on which product was purchased and how frequently the customer had previously ordered.

Retention became the growth engine.

Key Takeaway

Acquisition gets the first order. Lifecycle gets the next ten.

NOOMA's paid ads were already working. The missing piece was what happened after the click. By building a post-purchase system that educated, reminded, and nudged at the right moments, we turned one-time buyers into subscribers and cut the time between repeat orders by over 20%.
